About PayTracker

Your trusted partner in financial management and recurring payment tracking.

Our Mission

PayTracker was created to solve a universal problem: losing track of recurring payments. We believe everyone deserves a simple, secure way to manage their subscriptions and recurring expenses in one centralized location.

Key Features

  • Bank & Email Integration: Securely link your bank accounts and email to track every recurring payment automatically.
  • Real-time Alerts: Receive notifications before payments are due so you're always prepared.
  • Detailed Analytics: Understand your spending patterns with comprehensive reports and insights.
  • Multi-device Support: Access your dashboard anytime, anywhere on any device.
  • Security First: Your financial data is encrypted and protected with bank-level security.
  • Easy Management: Edit, pause, or cancel subscriptions directly from your dashboard.
